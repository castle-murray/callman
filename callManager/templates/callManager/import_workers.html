How to Export a VCF File

From iPhone:

  1. Open the Contacts app.
  2. Tap Lists at the top left, then long-press All Contacts.
  3. Select Export from the menu.
  4. Choose your desired fields (e.g., "Select All Fields") and tap Done.
  5. Use Mail or another app to send the .vcf file to your computer or email accessible on this device.

From Android:

  1. Open the Contacts app (or Google Contacts).
  2. Go to Settings or tap the menu (three dots or lines).
  3. Select Import/Export > Export to .vcf file.
  4. Choose where to save (e.g., internal storage) and confirm.
  5. Transfer the .vcf file to your computer or email via Gmail or another method.
